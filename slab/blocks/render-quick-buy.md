# Quick buy content


## Overview

A block for rendering a quick buy interface, allowing users to quickly purchase items without navigating away from the current page. It includes a loading animation and a button rendered from the 'c__button' snippet.


## Common use cases

- Ensure the 'c__button' snippet is properly configured to match the theme's design
- Adjust the gap size in theme settings to fit the design requirements
- Use in quick buy overlays or popups for fast product purchasing
- Display quick buy functionality in product cards or grids

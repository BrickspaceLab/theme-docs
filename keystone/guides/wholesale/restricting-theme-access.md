# Restricting Your Shopify Theme for Wholesale Ordering

## Keystone: Configuring wholesale access

***

### Summary

Configure your theme to restrict access to wholesale features and content for approved customers only.

***

### Access Account Settings

1. Open the **Theme Editor** by clicking **Customize** next to the relevant theme.
2. Click **Theme Settings** (gear icon near the top left).
3. Scroll down and click **Account**.
